Project Description
A lot of ideas, thought processes, and discussions went into my ZHdK portfolio. Therefore, I wanted to list it as its own project.

The portfolio is split into two parts - digital and physical. In order to create a sense of identity, I developed a visual guide throughout both parts.
Print
The booklet is the physical part of my portfolio. It gives a quick overview of what to expect, who I am and my motivations behind it. In the end it links to the main part - the digital portfolio. To enhance tactile and visual appeal, I opted for a flatbook design with an open back. I selected a high GSM for the pages to ensure a pleasant hand feel.
Web
Since most of my projects are interactive or animated, I've decided to create an online portfolio. For the implementation I used plain HTML, CSS and JavaScript. 3D Models were integrated using the model-viewer library.
