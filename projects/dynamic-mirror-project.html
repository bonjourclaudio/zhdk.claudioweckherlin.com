dynamic mirror

2023 personal project immersive
Project Description
This was my first time experimenting and creating an immersive project. I have always been fascinated by installations and experiences that truly captivate me â€” drawing me in and disconnecting me from the outside world.

While learning p5.js, I aimed to create an installation that did just that. I began experimenting with different inputs, graphical concepts, and methods to extend the visual into physical space, transforming the room into both canvas and medium.

By projecting everything onto randomly placed mirrors, I sought to introduce an element of unpredictability into the project, adding both visual interest and an immersive quality.

To enhance the sense of immersion further, I incorporated the auditory aspect. I created a base frequency that dynamically shapes itself based on the same analog inputs as the graphic. This integration makes the overall experience more complete and intense.
Process
In order to move and control the visual on every axis as well as modifying the frequencies and reverb being played, I've wired up a joystick to my Arduino board. The values are being passed by the Arduino software to the P5.js project.
